WhatsApp Lead Follow-Up Flow

A simple sequence you can adapt to follow up without sounding robotic.

Message 1: acknowledge the enquiry and restate the exact thing they showed interest in.
Message 2: send one useful piece of clarity, not a brochure dump.
Message 3: ask one easy yes/no question to move the conversation forward.
Message 4: use social proof or a practical detail to reduce hesitation.
Message 5: offer a small next step like a call, site visit, or shortlist.
Message 6: close politely if there is no response, but leave the door open.
