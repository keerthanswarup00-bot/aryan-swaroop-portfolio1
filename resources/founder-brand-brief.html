Founder Brand Brief

Answer these before you commission any branding work.

What does the business really sell beyond the obvious category?
Who is the buyer you actually want, not just anyone who can pay?
Why do people hesitate before buying from you?
What should someone feel in the first 5 seconds of seeing the brand?
Which competitors feel too generic, too premium, or too confusing?
What should never be true about the finished brand?
