Ad Creative Audit Sheet

Check a real estate ad against these filters before you spend money on it.

Can a buyer understand project type, area, and offer in under 3 seconds?
Is one strong message doing the job, instead of six competing lines?
Are dimensions, pricing, or practical hooks visible where needed?
Does the visual match the buyer’s trust threshold for that location and segment?
Does the CTA tell the buyer exactly what happens next?
Would this still work if the logo were removed and only the core message remained?
