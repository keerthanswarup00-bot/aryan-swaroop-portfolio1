Packaging Design Brief

Use this to brief packaging work with more clarity than “make it look premium.”

What shelf context or buying environment will this packaging compete in?
What is the single most important perception the pack must create?
Which product details must be instantly visible?
What cultural, regional, or emotional cues should the pack carry?
What should look proprietary to this brand, not category-generic?
What must the customer remember after seeing it once?
